# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-03-02*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** GWP Dispatch is Great Western Productions' branded press hub and editorial newsroom, publishing announcements and practitioner commentary that builds domain authority across the GWP ecosystem.
**What it does:** Publishes 2–4 posts per month across three editorial formats — short wire announcements, practitioner field reports, and industry commentary. Each post is engineered to create natural, editorially justified backlinks to GWP properties while building organic search visibility.
**Product category:** Branded content hub / editorial newsroom
**Product type:** Static Astro site (self-hosted)
**Business model:** Not a standalone product — serves as an SEO and authority-building node for Great Western Productions and its portfolio of properties.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** GWP properties lack a centralized editorial voice that ties together their work and creates natural SEO authority signals.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Social posts don't build domain authority or persist in search
- Generic press releases feel hollow and don't drive backlinks
- Scattered blog posts on individual properties don't compound

**What it costs them:** Without a content hub, GWP properties rank lower and lose organic discovery to competitors.
**Emotional tension:** Doing serious technical and creative work with no public record of it.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Individual property blogs — falls short because they don't cross-link or build shared authority
**Indirect:** Social media announcements — falls short because no SEO benefit, ephemeral
**Indirect:** PR/press agencies — falls short because generic, not practitioner-voiced

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Practitioner-authored, not generic agency content
- Deliberate linking architecture (1/3 to services, 1/3 to creative properties, 1/3 standalone)
- Covers real implementations: actual n8n workflows, actual print-on-demand failures and fixes

**How we do it differently:** Written by the people who built the systems, not content marketers describing them second-hand.
**Why that's better:** Builds credibility and earns editorial links, not manipulative ones.
**Why customers choose us:** N/A (editorial property)

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Another company blog nobody reads" | Three distinct editorial formats; linking architecture keeps it functional even with small audience |
| "Too thin to rank" | Focused on 300–800 word posts with genuine practitioner detail, not keyword stuffing |

**Anti-persona:** Readers looking for beginner tutorials or generic listicles — GWP Dispatch is practitioner-voiced and assumes competence.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** GWP properties have no unified content presence or editorial authority
**Pull:** Self-hosted Astro site, full control, no platform risk
**Habit:** Announcing work only via social (ephemeral, no SEO)
**Anxiety:** Content investment with slow/uncertain ROI on domain authority
