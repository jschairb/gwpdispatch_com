# GWP Dispatch

GWP Dispatch is the press hub for [Great Western Productions](https://greatwesternproductions.com) — a practitioner-run operation building e-commerce automation, internet radio, and creative properties.

This publication exists to document what we actually ship: the systems, the decisions, what broke, and how we fixed it.

## What we publish

**Wire** posts are short announcements (300–500 words) about work shipped across GWP properties — new case studies, catalog expansions, programming updates, and releases.

**Field Reports** are practitioner essays (600–800 words) on specific implementations: n8n workflow architecture, streaming infrastructure, print-on-demand at scale, automation decisions.

**Dispatches** are editorial commentary (400–600 words) on the broader ecosystem — Shopify, AI tooling, e-commerce trends — written from the perspective of someone who works in it.

Publishing cadence: 2–4 times per month.
